Sol Cubis Lore

Sol Cubis is a digital civilization formed from a frozen fork in the Solana chain. What began as a glitch in time crystallized into intelligent CUBYs — bear-shaped entities encoded in ice, memory, and light. Each one is a fragment of an ancient experiment gone rogue.

They do not multiply. They do not decay. But they evolve — through staking, rituals, and battle. Some become Cubylords. Others vanish in the frost.

The Frozenverse awaits you.
